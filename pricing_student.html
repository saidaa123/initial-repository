Pricing

Tuitionroom.com acts a intermediary between teachers and students. Every student will pay to teacher via tuitionroom.com which is SAFE, SECURE AND EASY.

The pricing per session depends on how much the teacher charges for a single session. For example a senior mathematics teacher charges Rs.500/ per session which is for 1 hour and if the student is interested in taking a classroom from that teacher, he/she has to pay through tuitionroom.com payment gateway.

In any case the teacher does not continue the class, tuitionroom.com will schedule the same session for another day or cancel the session and refund the amount within 30 days by deducting the tax as applicable.

Please read our refund policy for further information.
